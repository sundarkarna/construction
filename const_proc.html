CONSTRUCTION PROCESS

Soil Testing

Soil Test for building construction depends on properties of soil. Design of the foundation is based on soil test report. Soil test for construction is the basic step for building construction If soil was not tested correctly the whole building or structure is damaged or collapsed. So soil testing is very much importance for constructing building site.

BHOOMI POOJA

Bhoomi Pooja is a ritual performed in hounours of the Goddess bhoomi and vasthu purush. The purpose of Bhoomi Pooja is believed to eradicate the ill effects and vastu dos has in the land and pave the way for smooth construction. Bhoomi poojan is performed in the north east corner of construction site. The digging of site should begin in north east corner to ensure the corner level is always lower than the rest of construction site. It is allowing morning and constant light in the whole site.

COLUMN MARING

Column marking is the basic step before excavation the site. Mark the four sides of the given plot. Then mark the column as per the given 2 dimensional plan / Structural drawing. Column marking is very much importance for constructing site. If some deviations will occur inside of the room area will differ. It is the back bone for building construction

Column Excavation and column Foundation

After marking the columns as per given diagram, column excavation should done by use of JCB or hitachi or by means of labours. After excavation shuttering work to done and concrete the shuttering area. After one day fill the sand in excavated area and properly watered it. On top of the land plinth beam should be properly shuttered and all steel works should be done for concreting. Brick work should be done on the top of the beam. Finally measure the height with equal to road height. Again beam work should be done. Fill it with slurry dust or sand inside of foundation. Now its ready for constructing walls.

Roof shuttering works

After constructing the walls as per the given plan. Finally shuttering sheets to be placed on the roof for steel works. Before concreting the roof PVC pipes to be placed in the roof for electrical works. The area was ready for concrete work

Roof concreting

Using 20 mm docongraded aggregates in M20 mix as per the given structural drawings. After roof concreting it must be cured for min 21 days.If instant ready mix cement is used. It must be cured for min 15 days. After that all shuttering sheet to be removed.
